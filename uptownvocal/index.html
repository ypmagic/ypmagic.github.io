Uptown Vocal is Columbia University's premier jazz a cappella group. Since 1991, these cool cats have been bringing new and interesting takes on all of your favorite jazz standards. We have performed on college campuses, bars, hotels, cruise ships, morning shows and even on stage with Ben Folds!

New Album Release!

Posted on May 1, 2017

We are thrilled to announce the release of our newest album, UV. Featuring singers from the Class of 2015 to 2020, this eponymous album is a testament to our signature jazzy style. The album is available on streaming platforms such as iTunes and Spotify, and you can also order a physical copy to start listening today!
